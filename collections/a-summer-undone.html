A Summer Undone




for the breezes, the blues, and the beauty of not knowing what's coming next

So... Summer often feels awful. The vibes are great, but in reality, it's too hot, sticky, mosquito bites, ..., ugh. Regardless, each summer unfolds with a vibe that is both new and nostalgically alike as we grow and age. What does this look like in photos?
Here, not every photo has to say “wow.” Sometimes, it’s the story they tell together that truly matters.




with photography, let us chase summer’s promise— a breath held too long, caught between our hope in the bitter winters and quiet longing in the spring. these photos capture the space between dreaming of summer and living it...

In this collection, my key to creating a "Summer vibe" in photos is slightly lowering the contrast, and make sure there's no distracting background subjects. Soft blue sky, clean greens, puffy clouds— that's the spirit :)

Then comes that crisp, summery green—so vivid it lights up your eyes and fills your heart with hope. It carries you through the crisp autumn and biting winter, helping you wait for this moment to return next year.
