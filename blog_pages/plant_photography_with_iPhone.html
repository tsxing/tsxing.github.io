Plant Photography with iPhone

I have been photographing these small flowers, weeds, and clovers for a while, and I thought I'd note down some insights.

Photographing small flowers

I try to find an angle that makes the flower either centered or on one of the three dividing lines (rule of thirds). If a flower feels unflattering, it's usually due to the angle you're approaching it with. Generally, it may be easier to go for flowers that display more symmetry. For example, if you're looking to capture a "clean" shot, finding flowers that have "filmy petals" may not be the best option.
